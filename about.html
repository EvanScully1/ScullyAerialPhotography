The Creation

During the Covid-19 pandemic, over the winter in end of 2021 Scully Aerial Photography was established. Evan Scully, a junior in highschool at the time, got his Part 107 Commercial UAS License from Hanscom Air Field in Bedford, Massachusetts.

Background

"Remote controlled cars, boats, planes, and quadcopters were my favorite toys growing up. My goal every year starting at the age of 12 was to save up for a 'big' purchase on Black Friday. When I was an eighth grader, I bought a DJI Spark. Years, later when I decided I needed a better drone for my business, I upgraded to, now my current drone, the DJI Mavic 2 Pro with a Swedish camera called the Hasselblad L1D-20c camera." -Evan Scully

Locations

Local to Massachusetts, southern Rhode Island, North-Eastern parts of Connecticut, and southern parts of New Hampshire.

Payments

Payments are based on photoshoot/videoshoot type, length of video (if applicable), edits needed, travel, prints, or other requests that may be inquired and negotiated.
